## Objective
Enable loading golden Q&A datasets from both CSV and JSON formats, with validation and format normalization.

<read_first>
- kb_server/evaluation/dataset.py
- ingest/parsers/csv_parser.py (if exists, for CSV reading patterns)
</read_first>
<action>
Create `kb_server/evaluation/csv_loader.py` with:
